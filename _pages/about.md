<section class="cv-section">
  <h2>Biography</h2>

  <p>
    Dr. Liqiao Xia is currently a Research Assistant Professor at the
    <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/ise/" target="_blank">
      Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISE), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)
    </a>.
    Prior to joining ISE, he was a Visiting Research Fellow at the Vienna University of Technology, Austria,
    and the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany, and a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of
    Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Case Western Reserve University, USA.
  </p>

  <p>
    He received his Ph.D. degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from
    <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/" target="_blank">The Hong Kong Polytechnic University</a> in 2024,
    and was a Visiting Graduate Student at the Institute for Manufacturing,
    <a href="https://www.cam.ac.uk/" target="_blank">University of Cambridge</a>, UK.
  </p>

  <p>
    His primary research interests focus on data-driven intelligent manufacturing systems, including
    human-robot collaboration, industrial artificial intelligence, and prognostics and health management. He has published papers in leading journals such as
    TII, TMech, TRel, TIM, TBD, RESS, RCIM, JMS, and others.
  </p>
</section>
